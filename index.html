Handling

Simple thaw-and-use workflow

  1. Keep frozen at 0°F / -18°C or below.
  2. Thaw dough balls under refrigeration for 24 hours.
  3. Rest at room temperature for 30–40 minutes before stretching.
  4. Stretch by hand or equipment, top, and bake.
