Handling & Storage

Basic preparation guidance for kitchen teams

  1. Keep frozen at 0°F / -18°C or below.
  2. Transfer dough balls to refrigeration and thaw for approximately 24 hours.
  3. Allow dough to rest at room temperature for 30–40 minutes before stretching.
  4. Stretch by hand or equipment, top as desired, and bake according to oven platform.

Ingredients: 00 Flour, Water, Olive Oil, Salt, Yeast, Sugar, Ascorbic Acid, and L-Cysteine Hydrochloride.

Store frozen for long-term holding. Once thawed, refrigerated shelf life is up to 4 days under appropriate cold storage conditions.
